Aloo tikki

Aloo tikki recipe

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Peas
  • Corriander leaves
  • Rice flour
  • Green chillies
  • Chilli powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt

Method to cook

  1. Take a pressure pan, pour water and boil the potatoes.
  2. Let the potatoes cool down.Then mash the potatoes well.
  3. Add salt, chilli powder,garam masala,rice flour,peas,corriander leaf and green chillies.
  4. Mix all ingredients together.
  5. Make small size patties.
  6. Take a fry pan, pour some oil and heat it.
  7. Fry the patties.
  8. Aloo tikki is ready!
