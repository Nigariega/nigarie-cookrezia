Green gram laddu

Green gram laddu recipe

Ingredients

  • Green gram
  • Badam
  • Cardamom powder
  • Jaggery syrup
  • Water
  • Ghee

Method to cook

  1. Take the green gram and roast them in pan.
  2. Grind the fried green gram.Cool it down.
  3. For jaggery syrup, boil the water and add jaggeryand cardamom powder.Wait till you get thick syrup.
  4. To the green gram mixture add few spoon of ghee,badam,and jaggery syrup.
  5. Mix the together.
  6. Make small size balls.
  7. Green gram laddu is ready!
