Onion rings recipe

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Rice flour
  • Chilli powder
  • Maida
  • Garlic paste
  • Milk
  • Bread crumbs

Method to cook

  1. Take onion and cut the into rings.
  2. Take a bowl and add rice flour,maida,salt,pepper,garlic paste,chilli powder .
  3. Pour the milk and egg.
  4. Mix all ingredients together.
  5. Take the rings and dip into the mixture.
  6. coat them with the bread crumbs.
  7. Refrigerate it for 15 minutes.
  8. Take a fry pan, pour some oil and heat it.
  9. Fry the rings until they get golden brown in color.
  10. Onion rings is ready!
