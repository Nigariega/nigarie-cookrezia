Peas pulao

Peas pulao recipe

Ingredients

  • Cloves
  • Bay leaf
  • cardamom
  • cinnamon
  • star anise
  • Onion
  • Green chilli
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Salt
  • Peas
  • Basmathi rice
  • Coconut milk

Method to cook

  1. Take a pressure pan and ghee.Heat it up.
  2. Add the whole spices.
  3. Add the green chillies,onion,ginger garlic paste and salt.Fry it for 2 Minutes.
  4. Add peas and cook it for 5 minutes.
  5. Add basmathi rice.Stir it up.
  6. Pour the diluted coconut milk.Add salt if required.
  7. Keep the flame in high and wait for 4 whistles.
  8. Peas pulao is ready!
