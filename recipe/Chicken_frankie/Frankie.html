Chicken frankie

Chicken frankie recipe

Ingredients

  • Cooked chappathi
  • Sliced onions
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Minced chicken
  • Schezwan sauce

Method to cook

  1. Take the cooked chappathi.
  2. Apply some ketchup to it.
  3. For spicy mayonnaise, mix mayonnaise and schezwan sauce together.
  4. Apply the spicy mayonnaise.
  5. Top with sliced onions and minced chicken pieces.
  6. Add some mayonnaise on it.
  7. Wrap the frankie.
  8. Chappathi frankie is ready!
