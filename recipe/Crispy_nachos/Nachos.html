Crispy Nachos recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Onion
  • Maida
  • Cumin seeds
  • Garlic
  • Turmeric powder
  • Chaat masala
  • Chilli powder
  • Water
  • oil
  • Salt

Method to cook

  1. To a mixer jar, add cumin seeds,onion and garlic.Grind into fine paste.
  2. In a pan pour water and let it boil.
  3. Add salt,oil,paste,maida,rice flour,turmeric powder and mix it.
  4. Close with lid and cook it for 5 minutes.
  5. Transfer dough into bowl and kneed it well.
  6. Sprinkle some maida to the surface and place the dough .
  7. Flatten the dough and makes holes on it.
  8. Cut into triangle shape pieces.
  9. Take a pan and pour some oil.Heat it up.
  10. Add the nachos and fry it on both sides crispy.
  11. For masala topping, Mix chaat masala and chilli powder together.
  12. Mix masala with nachos.
  13. Crispy nachos is ready!
